Frequently Asked Questions

Is ViralVideos free?

Yes. ViralVideos is currently in free beta. Connect your TikTok account and start publishing daily horoscope videos at no cost.

What permissions does ViralVideos need?

We request user.info.basic (profile info for authentication) and video.publish (to upload videos to your account). We do not read your messages, access your followers, or post anything other than the horoscope videos you've authorized.

How do I disconnect?

You can revoke ViralVideos' access at any time from your TikTok settings: Security and permissions → Apps and sessions. All publishing stops immediately.

Are the videos labeled as AI-generated?

Yes. Every video published by ViralVideos includes TikTok's AIGC label. We are fully compliant with TikTok's AI-generated content policy.
